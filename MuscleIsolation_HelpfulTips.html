    • Helpful Tips for Identification

      Start by assessing what it is you do often: Do you run a lot? Squat a lot? Chest day? Whatever it is, I want you to determine what that activity utilizes when performed. Now it is simply a matter of finding the counter pulling muscles and utilizing this muscle isolation page to find the workouts best suited for your routine!

      Another approach you can take is to start by assessing where your joint pain is located. What hurts? By isolating the joint, you can take a look at what muscles surrounding the joint are actively exercised and which ones are neglected. After a little research it should be very apparent which exercises you need to begin including in your routine!

      Lastly, a great approach for those who really can't determine what exercise they need to include: When you perform the right exercise your body needs, the pain relief is almost immediate. Though temporary till properly strengthened, you will know if the exercise brings about any pain relief through simple trial and error going through our collective list of exercises for you to choose from!

    • Are YOU ready to LIVE PAIN FREE?

      Hello! Welcome to our App where we will help YOU begin your journey to a healthy and pain free body! Our goal is to educate and help people live pain-free lives. Our content gets detailed to help match individual scenarios from sedentary lifestyle with dull aches to avid athletes with joint pain. However, the solution is always the same, and that is exercise! How you should exercise to fix your type of pain is what we hope to teach you!

    Where to Start? No Worries!

      When looking through our side bar selections, please start with the educational section “Why am I in Pain?”to begin assessing what is the cause of your pain and where you should be looking to fix your pain! After you’ve identified what area to look in, select “My Routine Page” to begin building your own individualized program and begin living pain free!

    Injury Prevention Starts with Stretching!

      Did you know: Upwards of 80% of injuries in the military are due to Tendonitis. What is Tendonitis you may ask? Tendonitis is a fancy way of describing a sprain or strain on the muscles. Why does it happen so frequently? To answer that we must dive into why it happens at all! Sprains and Strains occur when the muscle is not properly stretched inbetween workouts. Stretching must be done while the muscles are warmed up, therefore the most effcient time is right after a work out. Back to our answer then! The military is often being rushed around and neglects to take the extra 10-20 minutes after a workout to stretch. Don't become a statistic! Use this list of stretches to build yourself a routine for after your workouts!

    Create a Balanced Body!

      In most areas of our lives we find ourselves specializing or emphisizing favoritism in a specific area. The same goes for our muscles! Whether that is a favorite sport or loving chest day a little too much, there is a link between that favoritism and the pain you feel! Think of your body as a mechanism full of pulling muscles and counter pulling muscles. Your biceps curl your arm and your triceps straigten it. Your Hamstrings curl your leg and your Quads straigten it. Your Abductors lift your leg out and your Adductors pull it back in. These pulls and counter pulls exist all throughout your body! Here we hope to identify what pulls you have specialized in so that we may find the counter pulls to strengthen and bring balance to your joint and relieve the pain!
